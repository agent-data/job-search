# Job Preferences Brief

**Summary:** Senior product designer with ~8 years in B2B SaaS, looking for a design role at a
small-to-mid product company where design has a real seat at the table. Wants to own end-to-end product
work — not just push pixels — and to mentor more junior designers. Remote within the US, or hybrid in
the Pacific Northwest.

## Must-haves / dealbreakers
- A product design role (UX/product), not visual/marketing/brand design.
- Remote within the US, OR hybrid based in the Portland or Seattle area. (Fully onsite outside the PNW is a dealbreaker.)
- Designers are embedded with engineering and product — not a downstream "make it pretty" service team.

## Strong preferences
- Senior or lead level with real ownership of a product area end to end (research → ship).
- A company small enough that design has direct influence on product direction.
- A healthy design culture: design system in place or actively being built, regular critique, research valued.

## Nice-to-haves
- Some front-end fluency expected or welcomed (can prototype in code / read the codebase).
- Mentorship of junior designers, or a path toward a design-lead / manager track if I want it later.
- Mission I can get behind — climate, education, healthcare, or developer tools.

## Red flags
- "Unicorn" listings that bundle product design + brand + marketing + front-end into one underpaid seat.
- No existing design practice and no plan to build one (sole designer with no support or budget).
- Heavy agency-style billable-hours or client-services framing rather than owning a product.
