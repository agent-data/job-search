# Job Preferences Brief

**Summary:** Backend engineer who wants data-intensive systems work at a mid-size company, remote-first, with a sane on-call load.

## Must-haves / dealbreakers
- Backend or platform engineering (not frontend-focused).
- Remote within the US.
- No more than light, shared on-call.

## Strong preferences
- Go or Rust; distributed systems.
- A team that ships weekly and writes design docs.

## Nice-to-haves
- Open-source culture.

## Red flags
- "Rockstar/ninja" ads; permanent crunch.
